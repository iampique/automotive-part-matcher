================================================================================
1.0 SCOPE
================================================================================

This specification defines the requirements for an electrical connector system
designed to interconnect automotive infotainment head units with high-resolution
display modules. The connector shall provide reliable transmission of power,
video signals (LVDS), and control data in a cost-effective package suitable
for high-volume automotive production.

================================================================================
2.0 APPLICATION
================================================================================

The connector is intended for use in:
- Infotainment head unit to display panel interface
- Dashboard display module connections
- Center console display interconnections
- Video signal and power distribution

================================================================================
3.0 ELECTRICAL REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

3.1 Contact Configuration
    - Total number of contacts: 48 pins
    - Power contacts: 8 contacts (4 pairs for power/ground)
    - Signal contacts: 40 contacts (LVDS differential pairs and control)

3.2 Voltage Rating
    - Operating voltage: 24V DC nominal
    - Voltage range: 9V to 32V DC (per ISO 7637-2)
    - Maximum continuous voltage: 36V DC
    - Dielectric withstand voltage: 500V DC (1 minute)
    - Creepage distance: 2.5mm minimum
    - Clearance distance: 2.0mm minimum

3.3 Current Rating
    - Power contacts: 3A per contact maximum
    - Total power capacity: 12A maximum
    - Signal contacts: 0.5A maximum per contact
    - Continuous duty rating at maximum operating temperature

3.4 LVDS Signal Requirements
    - LVDS pairs: 8 differential pairs (16 contacts)
    - Signal voltage: 350mV differential (typical)
    - Common mode voltage: 1.2V
    - Data rate: 1.5 Gbps per lane minimum
    - Signal integrity: Eye diagram compliance per LVDS standard
    - Skew: < 100ps between differential pair members
    - Impedance: 100Ω differential impedance (±10%)

3.5 EMI Shielding Requirements
    - Shielding effectiveness: > 40dB at frequencies 100MHz to 3GHz
    - Shield continuity: < 10mΩ resistance across mated connector
    - Shield termination: 360-degree shield contact required
    - EMI gasket: Integrated EMI gasket or conductive housing required
    - Ground contacts: Dedicated ground contacts for shield connection

================================================================================
4.0 MECHANICAL REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

4.1 Connector Housing
    - Material: Engineering thermoplastic (UL94 V-0 or better)
    - Color: Black (standard automotive color)
    - Locking mechanism: Positive locking with retention clip
    - Mating force: < 30N
    - Unmating force: 15N - 50N (with retention clip released)
    - Connector height: < 15mm above PCB surface

4.2 Contact Design
    - Contact material: Phosphor bronze or beryllium copper
    - Contact finish: Selective gold plating (0.38μm minimum) or tin plating
    - Contact retention: Minimum 30N pull-out force per contact
    - Contact resistance: < 10mΩ per contact (initial)
    - Contact resistance stability: < 20mΩ after 100 mating cycles

4.3 PCB Mounting
    - Mounting style: Surface mount (SMT) or through-hole
    - Solder joint reliability: Per IPC-A-610 Class 2 minimum
    - Coplanarity: < 0.1mm for SMT contacts

================================================================================
5.0 ENVIRONMENTAL REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

5.1 Operating Temperature
    - Continuous operating range: -30°C to +85°C
    - Storage temperature: -40°C to +100°C
    - Temperature cycling: 500 cycles (-30°C to +85°C)
    - Operating temperature with derating: Full performance to +85°C

5.2 Environmental Sealing
    - Ingress Protection: IP67 minimum
    - IP67 test conditions: Immersion in 1 meter of water for 30 minutes
    - Dust protection: IP6X (complete dust ingress protection)
    - Sealing method: Gasket, potting, or overmold acceptable

5.3 Humidity
    - Relative humidity: 85% RH at 85°C (500 hours)
    - Damp heat cycling: 10 cycles (25°C to 85°C, 85% RH)

5.4 Vibration and Shock
    - Vibration: Per ISO 16750-3, 20-2000 Hz, 0.1g²/Hz
    - Shock: 50g, 11ms half-sine (per ISO 16750-2)
    - Performance: No electrical discontinuity during testing

================================================================================
6.0 CERTIFICATION AND COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

6.1 Quality Management

================================================================================
9.0 TECHNICAL NOTES AND DESIGN CONSIDERATIONS
================================================================================

9.1 LVDS Signal Integrity
    - Maintain controlled impedance throughout signal path
    - Minimize via transitions in PCB design
    - Ensure proper ground return paths for differential signals
    - Consider signal pre-emphasis or equalization if cable length > 500mm

9.2 EMI Mitigation
    - Shielding must provide continuous coverage around all signal contacts
    - Ground contacts should be distributed throughout connector
    - Consider ferrite cores for cable assembly if needed
    - Connector housing should provide EMI gasket compression

9.3 Thermal Management
    - Consider power dissipation in connector design
    - Ensure adequate airflow around connector in application
    - Thermal derating may be required at maximum temperature

9.4 Assembly Considerations
    - Design for automated assembly preferred
    - Provide assembly fixtures or guides if needed
    - Clear visual indicators for proper mating orientation
    - Color coding or keying to prevent incorrect mating
