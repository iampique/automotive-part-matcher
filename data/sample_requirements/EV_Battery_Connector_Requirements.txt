================================================================================
1.0 SCOPE
================================================================================

This document specifies the technical requirements for a high-voltage connector
system designed for Electric Vehicle (EV) Battery Management System (BMS)
applications. The connector shall provide reliable electrical interconnection
between the battery pack modules and the BMS control unit, ensuring safe
operation under extreme environmental conditions.

================================================================================
2.0 APPLICATION
================================================================================

The connector is intended for use in:
- Battery pack module interconnections
- BMS control unit to battery pack interface
- High-voltage DC power distribution
- Battery monitoring and control signal transmission

================================================================================
3.0 ELECTRICAL REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

3.1 Contact Configuration
    - Total number of contacts: 120 pins minimum
    - Contact arrangement: Mixed power and signal contacts
    - Power contacts: 40 contacts minimum
    - Signal contacts: 80 contacts minimum

3.2 Voltage Rating
    - Operating voltage: 600V DC minimum
    - Maximum continuous voltage: 800V DC
    - Dielectric withstand voltage: 3000V DC (1 minute, no breakdown)
    - Creepage distance: 8mm minimum
    - Clearance distance: 6mm minimum

3.3 Current Rating
    - Current per contact: 10A minimum (power contacts)
    - Signal contacts: 2A maximum
    - Total connector current capacity: 400A minimum
    - Continuous duty rating at maximum temperature

3.4 CAN Bus Requirements
    - Dedicated CAN bus contacts: 4 contacts (CAN-H, CAN-L, CAN-GND, Shield)
    - CAN bus signal integrity: 120Ω termination capability
    - Data rate: 500 kbps minimum
    - Signal rise/fall time: < 50ns
    - Common mode rejection: > 20dB

================================================================================
4.0 MECHANICAL REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

4.1 Connector Housing
    - Material: High-temperature engineering thermoplastic (UL94 V-0)
    - Color: Orange (per IEC 60417-5036 high-voltage identification)
    - Locking mechanism: Positive locking with audible click
    - Mating force: < 50N
    - Unmating force: 20N - 80N (with locking mechanism released)

4.2 Contact Design
    - Contact material: Copper alloy with selective gold plating
    - Contact retention: Minimum 50N pull-out force per contact
    - Contact finish: 0.76μm minimum gold over nickel barrier
    - Contact resistance: < 5mΩ per contact (initial)
    - Contact resistance stability: < 10mΩ after 1000 mating cycles

4.3 Vibration Resistance
    - Random vibration: 20-2000 Hz, 0.1g²/Hz (per ISO 16750-3)
    - Sinusoidal vibration: 10-2000 Hz, 10g peak
    - Duration: 22 hours per axis (X, Y, Z)
    - Performance: No electrical discontinuity > 1μs during vibration

4.4 Mechanical Durability
    - Mating cycles: 500 cycles minimum
    - Operating life: 15 years or 300,000 km vehicle life

================================================================================
5.0 ENVIRONMENTAL REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

5.1 Operating Temperature
    - Continuous operating range: -40°C to +150°C
    - Storage temperature: -55°C to +175°C
    - Temperature cycling: 1000 cycles (-40°C to +150°C)
    - Thermal shock: 10 cycles (-40°C to +150°C, 5 minute dwell)

5.2 Environmental Sealing
    - Ingress Protection: IP69K minimum
    - IP69K test conditions: 80°C water, 80-100 bar, 14-16 L/min, 30 seconds
    - Dust protection: IP6X (complete dust ingress protection)
    - Sealing material: Silicone rubber gaskets (permanent set < 20%)

5.3 Salt Spray Resistance
    - Test standard: ISO 9227 (NSS - Neutral Salt Spray)
    - Duration: 96 hours minimum
    - Concentration: 5% NaCl solution
    - Performance: No visible corrosion, no electrical degradation

5.4 Humidity
    - Relative humidity: 95% RH at 85°C (1000 hours)
    - Damp heat cycling: 10 cycles (25°C to 85°C, 95% RH)
