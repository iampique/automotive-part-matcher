1.0 SCOPE
================================================================================

This document specifies the technical requirements for a safety-critical
electrical connector system designed for Brake Electronic Control Module
(ECM) applications. This connector is classified as ASIL-D (Automotive Safety
Integrity Level D) per ISO 26262 and must meet the highest standards of
reliability, quality, and functional safety. Failure of this connector could
result in loss of vehicle control and serious injury or death.

================================================================================
2.0 APPLICATION
================================================================================

The connector is intended for use in:
- Brake Electronic Control Module (ECM) to vehicle harness interface
- Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) control unit connections
- Electronic Stability Control (ESC) system interconnections
- Brake actuator and sensor signal transmission
- Safety-critical power and signal distribution

================================================================================

================================================================================

3.1 Safety Classification

    - Safety integrity level: ASIL-D (highest level)

3.2 Safety Analysis Requirements

3.3 Diagnostic Capabilities
    - Built-in diagnostic features for contact integrity monitoring

================================================================================
4.0 ELECTRICAL REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

4.1 Contact Configuration
    - Total number of contacts: 24 pins
    - Power contacts: 6 contacts (3 pairs for power/ground)
    - Signal contacts: 18 contacts (sensor signals, control signals, diagnostics)

4.2 Voltage Rating
    - Operating voltage: 12V DC nominal
    - Voltage range: 6V to 16V DC (per ISO 7637-2)
    - Maximum continuous voltage: 18V DC
    - Dielectric withstand voltage: 1000V DC (1 minute, no breakdown)
    - Creepage distance: 4mm minimum
    - Clearance distance: 3mm minimum

4.3 Current Rating
    - Current per contact: 5A minimum (power contacts)
    - Signal contacts: 2A maximum per contact
    - Total connector current capacity: 15A minimum
    - Continuous duty rating at maximum operating temperature
    - Overcurrent protection: Must withstand 2x rated current for 1 minute

4.4 Contact Resistance
    - Initial contact resistance: < 5mΩ per contact
    - Contact resistance after 1000 cycles: < 10mΩ
    - Contact resistance stability: < 2mΩ variation over lifetime
    - High resistance detection: System must detect > 50mΩ per contact

================================================================================
5.0 MECHANICAL REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

5.1 Connector Housing
    - Material: High-performance engineering thermoplastic (UL94 V-0)
    - Color: Red (safety-critical identification) or as specified
    - Locking mechanism: Dual-lock system (primary and secondary locks)
    - Mating force: < 40N
    - Unmating force: 50N - 120N (with both locks released)
    - Lock retention: Locks must withstand 200N pull force

5.2 Contact Design
    - Contact material: High-reliability copper alloy
    - Contact finish: Gold plating minimum 0.76μm over nickel barrier
    - Contact retention: Minimum 80N pull-out force per contact
    - Contact design: Redundant contact design preferred for critical signals
    - Contact alignment: Self-aligning design to prevent damage during mating

5.3 Mechanical Durability
    - Mating cycles: 1000 cycles minimum
    - Operating life: 20 years or 500,000 km vehicle life
    - No degradation in electrical or mechanical performance over lifetime

5.4 Vibration and Shock Resistance
    - Random vibration: 20-2000 Hz, 0.1g²/Hz (per ISO 16750-3)
    - Sinusoidal vibration: 10-2000 Hz, 15g peak
    - Duration: 22 hours per axis (X, Y, Z)
    - Shock: 50g, 11ms half-sine (per ISO 16750-2)
    - Performance: Zero electrical discontinuity > 1μs during vibration/shock

================================================================================
6.0 ENVIRONMENTAL REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

6.1 Operating Temperature
    - Continuous operating range: -40°C to +125°C
    - Storage temperature: -55°C to +150°C
    - Temperature cycling: 2000 cycles (-40°C to +125°C)
    - Thermal shock: 20 cycles (-40°C to +125°C, 5 minute dwell)
    - Performance: Full electrical and mechanical performance throughout range

6.2 Environmental Sealing
    - Ingress Protection: IP68 minimum, IP69K preferred
    - IP68 test conditions: Immersion in 1.5 meters of water for 24 hours
    - IP69K test conditions: 80°C water, 80-100 bar, 14-16 L/min, 30 seconds
    - Dust protection: IP6X (complete dust ingress protection)
    - Sealing material: High-performance silicone rubber (permanent set < 15%)
    - Sealing reliability: Maintain seal integrity over full operating life

6.3 Chemical Resistance
    - Automotive fluids: Resistant to brake fluid, engine oil, transmission fluid
    - Test duration: 1000 hours immersion at 85°C
    - Performance: No degradation of materials or sealing

6.4 Salt Spray Resistance
    - Test standard: ISO 9227 (NSS - Neutral Salt Spray)
    - Duration: 240 hours minimum
    - Concentration: 5% NaCl solution
    - Performance: No visible corrosion, no electrical degradation

6.5 Humidity
    - Relative humidity: 95% RH at 85°C (2000 hours)
    - Damp heat cycling: 20 cycles (25°C to 85°C, 95% RH)
    - Condensation: Must operate with condensation present
